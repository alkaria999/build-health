Arms (Mon/Wed/Fri)

  • Bicep Curls: 3x12 (6 min)
  • Tricep Dips: 3x12 (6 min)
  • Shoulder Press: 3x12 (6 min)
  • Plank: 3x45 sec (4 min)

Legs/Thighs (Tue/Thu/Sat)

  • Squats: 3x15 (6 min)
  • Lunges: 3x12 each leg (6 min)
  • Wall Sit: 3x45 sec (4 min)
  • Glute Bridge: 3x15 (5 min)
  • Calf Raises: 3x20 (5 min)

  • Hydrate: Drink 2.5–3 liters of water daily.
  • Protein: Aim for 1.6–1.8 g per kg body weight.
  • Progressive overload: Gradually increase weights/reps.
  • Sleep: Ensure 7–8 hours of quality rest.
  • Track progress: Weight, arm & thigh circumference, strength gains.
